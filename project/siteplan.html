Student Life Guide

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Student Life Guide

This name was chosen because the website is designed to help university students organize their academic life and improve their study habits.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help university students manage their academic responsibilities by providing study resources and an interactive task planner.

Scenarios

  • How can I organize my daily study tasks?
  • What are some effective study tips for university students?

Color Scheme

The selected colors for this website are:

  • Blue: Used for headings and navigation
  • Light Gray: Used for the background
  • Gold: Used for accents and highlights

Typography

  • Poppins: Used for headings
  • Arial: Used for body text
